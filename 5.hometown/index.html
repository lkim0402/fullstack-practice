Visit Yongin!

Immerse yourself in the vibrant spirit of Yongin — a city that celebrates both history and creativity.

Top three activities to do at Yongin

Everland

Visit one of Korea's largest theme park with a variety of attractions, seasonal festivals, and themed zones.

Korean Folk Village

Experience Korea’s past through traditional homes, performances, and crafts at the Korean Folk Village.

Caribbean Bay

Enjoy thrilling slides, wave pools, and relaxing spas at Korea’s largest water park, Caribbean Bay.

Your guide

Your guide for Yongin is Joayong! The name "Joayong" is a combination of the SNS slang term "joayo" (좋아요), which means "I like it" or "like," and the "yong" (龍), which is the Chinese character for "dragon" found in the city's name, Yongin (용인).
